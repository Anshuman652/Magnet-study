Noun



Definition- a noun is a naming word. It can name anything: a person, animal, place, thing, quality, feeling or idea.

Type of noun

1. Common nouns are nouns that name any thing that is shared by every person or thing of the same class. they do not refer to any particular person or thing.
For example- tent, arms, shoulders, boys, cook, door.

2. proper nouns are nouns that name a particular person or thing. proper nouns could be our name, the name of our friends, the name of our school, the name of our building, the name of our state, the name of our country.

3. Collective nouns are nouns and that name a group of collection of things of the same kind.
For example- bouquet, class, bunch, crowd.
4. abstract nouns are nouns that name idea thoughts and feelings that a person can be feel with in himself but cannot experience through his five senses, that is, thought slight, smell, hearing, touch or taste.
5. Material noun these are nouns that have a material a substance and ingredient that other thing are made from.
Noun---- countable and uncountable
Nouns can also be countable or uncountable
Countable nouns are those that can be counted----nouns like fingers, flowers, desks, children, can be counted whereas nouns like water, milk, rice, sugar cannot be counted. We can use numbers for countable noun.



Verb



Definition - a word used to describe an action, state, or occurrence, and forming the main part of the predicate of a sentence, such as hear, become, happen.

Kinds of verbs

Transitive and intransitive verbs

1. Those verbs that are are followed by an object are known as transitive verbs.
2. Those verbs that do not need an object after them are known as intransitive verbs.

Finite and non-finite verbs based on whether verb change when the object or tense of sentence change, it can be classified as a finite or a non-finite verb.

For example:-

---->Chitra sings dum National anthem during the assembly. (Simple present tense)
---->Chitra Sang the national anthem during the assembly. (Simple past tense)
---->Chitram will sing the national anthem during the assembly. (Simple future tense)
